What personality should ProVibe have?

This will guide typography, colors, animations, and overall design direction

A

Energetic & Bold

Vibe: High energy, vibrant, youth-focused

Inspires: TikTok, Instagram, Behance

Design: Bright gradients, dynamic animations, bold typography, asymmetric layouts

Best for: Gen Z creators, viral content, fast-paced platform

B

Premium & Sleek

Vibe: Professional, sophisticated, aspirational

Inspires: Linear, Stripe, Apple

Design: Muted colors, elegant typography, smooth micro-interactions, generous spacing

Best for: Professional creators, B2B brand partnerships, premium positioning

C

Warm & Community-Driven

Vibe: Friendly, inclusive, human-centered

Inspires: Notion, Airbnb, Figma

Design: Soft colors, rounded corners, approachable typography, organic shapes

Best for: Local events, meetups, grassroots creator communities

D

Creative & Editorial

Vibe: Artistic, distinctive, magazine-like

Inspires: Awwwards winners, design studios, fashion sites

Design: Unique typography, grid-breaking layouts, dramatic spacing, custom cursors

Best for: Standing out, design-forward creators, brand differentiation

Your requirements mention inspiration from Stripe, Linear, Vercel, Notion — these lean toward B (Premium & Sleek), but you could push in a different direction to stand out in the creator space.
